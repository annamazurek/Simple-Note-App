Too many thoughts?

Write down your worries, ideas etc. It will help you to organize your thoughts and make a decision. You can ask yourself some questions to solve your problems.

  • Why is it a problem?
  • Why do you want to solve it? What it will give you in the future?
  • What you can do to solve it? Write down your options.
  • Can someone help you? Who and how?
  • How can you achieve your goal? What do you need?
  • When do you want to achieve this goal?
